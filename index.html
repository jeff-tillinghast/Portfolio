About Me

I'm deeply passionate about helping people solving problems through technology. As a school technology leader, that's taken me everywhere from curriculum design and teacher coaching, to launching and maintaining 200+ user WordPress Multi-User sites, to writing custom APIs and teaching Robotics and Engineering. I'm a voracious lifelong learner, as evidenced by the skills I've built through my own learning and problem-solving: building my first web server to run an application I needed for the classroom, learning SQL in order to effectively mantain our mission-critical Student Information System, learning Computer Science and Electrical Engineering in order to teach Robotics, and studying DevOps in order to help people and organizations innovate effectively, efficiently, and reliably.
